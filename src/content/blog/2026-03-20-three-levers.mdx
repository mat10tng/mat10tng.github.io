<div slot="insight">
## Principle
Agent deviation is rarely a code bug. Diagnosis localizes to three structural levers.
</div>
</Section>

<Section>
<div slot="insight">
## 1. The Prompt (Instruction)
Insufficient instruction or lack of explicit prohibition. 
</div>

<div slot="technical">
**Diagnostic Fix:**
Establish mandatory rules in project **System Rules** (e.g. `CLAUDE.md`).
```markdown
- **Rule:** You MUST verify build.
- **Rule:** Do NOT use library X.
```
</div>
</Section>

<Section>
<div slot="insight">
## 2. The Tools (Capability)
Structural ambiguity caused by redundant or overlapping tools.
</div>

<div slot="technical">
**Diagnostic Fix:**
- Merge overlapping tools.
- Rename for precision.
</div>
</Section>

<Section>
<div slot="insight">
## 3. The Constraints (Environment)
System limits preventing completion (e.g., small iteration budgets).
</div>

<div slot="technical">
**Diagnostic Fix:**
Automate bookkeeping and raise system budgets in `.env`.
```env
MAX_ITERATIONS=15
```
</div>
</Section>

<Section>
<div slot="insight">
## Order
Diagnosis localizes to structural areas in this sequence:
1. **Instruction** → 2. **Tooling** → 3. **System Limits**.
</div>
</Section>
